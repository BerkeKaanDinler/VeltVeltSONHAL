## Description (App Store + Play Store — up to 4000 chars)

**The fitness app for people who actually train.**

VELT is a premium workout tracker built for serious lifters. Log every set, every rep, every PR — and let the AI coach turn that data into smarter sessions.

**WHAT'S INSIDE**

▸ **90+ Exercises with Coaching Cues**
Step-by-step form instructions and coach tips for every movement. No more guessing what "scapular retraction" means.

▸ **10 Prebuilt Programs**
Push Pull Legs, StrongLifts 5×5, 5/3/1 by Wendler, Upper/Lower, GZCLP, nSuns, Beginner Full Body, and more. One tap to add them to your routines.

▸ **Active Workout, Reimagined**
Big tap targets. Tabular numbers. Rest timer with auto-start. Plate calculator. Set type badges (warmup, drop, failure). Superset grouping. Exercise swap mid-workout. RPE per set.

▸ **Personal Record Celebrations**
Beat your bench? Confetti. Hit a new squat? Heavy haptic. VELT remembers every PR across your training history.

▸ **Nutrition Tracking That Doesn't Suck**
Water tracker. Quick-log carousel for common foods. Live macros vs target. Weekly consistency view.

▸ **Progress Charts You'll Actually Read**
Volume by week / month / year. Muscle group balance. Bodyweight trend. Personal records timeline. Period-over-period delta.

▸ **5 Premium Themes**
Iron Dark, Warm Paper, Slate Mono, Rose Gold, Emerald Premium. Make VELT look like yours.

▸ **Apple Health Integration**
Automatically logs your workouts to Apple Health when you finish a session.

**VELT PRO — The Complete Experience**

VELT Pro unlocks the AI Nutritionist (weekly meal plans tailored to your goals), AI Coach Insights (plateau alerts, deload timing, recovery score), premium themes, unlimited history, CSV/JSON export, and custom multi-week programs.

Start with a 7-day free trial. Cancel anytime — no charge until day 8.
