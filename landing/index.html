What's inside

Built for the serious lifter.

Every detail tuned so logging a set is faster than typing into Notes — and your data turns into actual insight.

90+ Exercises

Form cues and coach tips for every movement. Filter by muscle, equipment, or difficulty. Quick-start any exercise as a single-move session.

10 Prebuilt Programs

PPL, StrongLifts 5×5, 5/3/1, U/L, GZCLP, nSuns, and more. One tap to add — VELT preserves weights and PRs across sessions.

Rest Timer with Auto-Start

Mark a set done — timer fires instantly. Background notifications keep you on time even when the phone is in your pocket.

Plate Calculator

Tell VELT the bar weight — get the plate breakdown per side. Color-coded, IPF-correct, works in kg or lb.

Supersets & RPE

Group exercises A1/A2 with one tap. Log RPE/RIR per set (optional). Track exactly what Hevy and Strong let you track — and more.

Personal Record Tracking

VELT remembers every PR across your history. Hit one mid-session? Confetti, haptic, and a celebration overlay.

Progress That Means Something

Weekly/monthly/yearly volume. Muscle group balance heatmap. Bodyweight trend. Period-over-period delta.

Nutrition Without the Spam

Water tracker, quick-log carousel, live macros vs target. No ads, no shopping links, no weight-loss BS.
